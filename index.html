Model Mars

Our young New Life Investigators fictitiously live in a settlement on Mars. In an effort to survive, they must learn ethics, social norms and science. Developed in partnership with The Travelling Telescope. Visit the virtual exhibition.

The Solar Grid

As a demo for our course Energy Sources of the Future, we raised funds and purchased a solar kit. We had a practical session on how to assemble the kit. The kit now helps run the servers at TunapandaNET. Mel continues to preach the gospel of renewable energy.

Space Saturdays

We are developing free (libre) space education for young adults in school. We shall deliver regular sessions of science lessons to inspire and engage. In partnership with the awesome folks at Leo Sky Africa, AASK, and MSK . Upcoming. Wanna sign up your school?

The Junto Club

Talk is cheap. So we do lot's of it. Benjamin Franklin would be proud. Every couple of weeks, we meet and discuss art, science, culture, and more. Subscribe to our calendar to join in. To have a taste of it, have a look at notes from a previous Junto.
